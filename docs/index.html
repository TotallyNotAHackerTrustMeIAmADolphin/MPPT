Calibration Wizard

This wizard will guide you through calibrating the voltage and current sensors. Please ensure you have a variable power supply and an electronic load ready.

Note: System safety limits are still active, but may trigger early if sensors are way out of calibration.

Step 1: Voltage Calibration (Low Point)

Connect your power supply to both the input and output terminals.

Adjust the supply to exactly 15.0 V (or close to it).

Vin Raw: 0 | Vout Raw: 0

Step 2: Voltage Calibration (High Point)

Increase your power supply voltage to exactly 24.0 V (or close to it).

Vin Raw: 0 | Vout Raw: 0

Safety: Change Wiring

STOP! Before proceeding to current calibration, you MUST change your wiring.

1. Disconnect the bridge between Input and Output terminals.

2. Connect your power supply to the Input terminals.

3. Connect your electronic load to the Output terminals.

The MOSFETs are currently OFF for your safety.

Step 3: Current Calibration (Low Point)

Connect supply to Input and an electronic load to Output.

The device is now in Pass-through mode (High-side MOSFETs ON).

Set your load to exactly 0.50 A (or close to it).

Ain Raw: 0 | Aout Raw: 0

Connect supply to Input and an electronic load to Output.
